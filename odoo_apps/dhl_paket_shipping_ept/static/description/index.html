Is this app compatible with Odoo Enterprise as well as Community?

Yes, the app works perfectly well with Odoo Enterprise (On-premise and Odoo.SH) as well as Community. Odoo Online (Cloud) does not allow installation of third-party apps and hence this app cannot be installed on Odoo Online.

I need an extra feature in this app. How can I request it?

You can contact us to request a customization in the app.

Do I get free upgrades and support if I purchase the app?

Yes, you can avail a free upgrade & support services for the app. Kindly read our support policy to know the details.

Can I cancel the shipment after Delivery Order is processed & label is generated?

Yes. You can cancel the shipment after your Delivery Order is processed. Just click on 'Cancel' button beside tracking number in Additional Info tab of Sale Order. If DHL Paket has shipped your packages to customer, then it will not be possible to cancel the shipment, practically.

Can I create product-specific shipping fees?

No. You cannot create product-specific shipping fees from DHL Paket.

What if I am not able to Retrieve Services?

You may need to recheck the credentials you entered while configuring shipping provider. It is also possible that there can be an address mismatch between DHL Paket & default company address entered in Odoo. In addition, you can also contact the support team of respective shipping provider.Although, it is not mandatory to always retrieve the shipping services. You can directly create delivery method from Sales / Configuration / Delivery Methods.

Can I change the delivery address after Delivery Order is processed?

No, it is not possible to change the recipient address once your Delivery Order is processed in Odoo.

I want to create some demo shipment before I use it real-time in Odoo. Does DHL Paket (Intraship) provides the credential for Test Environment?

Yes. DHL Paket Intraship provides Test Environment credentials in its user portal for testing purpose in order to carry out flawless shipment in Odoo.

I have validated the Delivery Order in Odoo. Why am I not able to see the shipping label in attachment section?

In DHL Paket Intraship, when you validate the Delivery Order, you need to enter certain recipient information manually to generate the shipping label and tracking number. Click on ‘Send to Shipper’ button after validating Delivery Order, enter all the required values in fields, click on ‘Send to Ship’ and the shipping label is generated in attachment section by DHL Paket API.

Do I need to create separate instances for Domestic & International services of DHL Paket?

No, Domestic & International services will be available in the same instance. There is no need to create a separate instance for each of them.

What is Weight of Shipping?

If you prefer to make packages in Odoo and put one or more products in the packages, the packaging weight will be calculated separately and then added to the actual weight of the Product. The total weight (product + package) is termed as 'Weight of Shipping'.

If I pack the whole order into multiple packages, will I get the shipping label for individual package?

Yes, you will get the shipping label for each individual package. Shipping Label will also be generated for products which are a part of order but not included in the packages.

How can the customers see & choose the DHL Paket Shipping Services on my Odoo Website?

At the time of checkout, the customer will see a list of DHL Paket services (Delivery Methods) which are marked as ‘Published on Website’ in Odoo. They will also be able to see the rates associated with each delivery method and choose whichever option they find appropriate.

How a customer can track the order from Odoo Website?

Customer can track their latest shipment status from their account in website under the My Account / Order section. Moreover, when Delivery Order is validated, customer will get an Email of tracking link if ‘Auto Send Shipment Mail’ is checked while configuring shipping instance.

I am shipping the products outside the country. Will it be possible to get Commercial Invoice for international shipment given by DHL Paket through this app?

By default you can get the Commercial Invoice for international shipment provided by DHL Paket in your Odoo.

I have a multiple account of DHL Paket. Can I configure all the accounts in this single app?

This connector will help you manage multiple DHL Paket accounts in a single Odoo Instance. You can create individual Shipping Instances for each of your DHL Paket accounts and manage various operations from a single Odoo environment.
